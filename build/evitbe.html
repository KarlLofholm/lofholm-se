Evitbe Eventsites Webapp, 2013, At Mogul

Introduction

Evitbe is a platform for customer communications and event management. It’s used by several large companies in Sweden to create booking sites, newsletters, customer surveys or handle most aspects of an event. Evitbe Eventsites are mobile versions of the booking sites, designed for smartphone use and with added information and functionality.
