Uppsalahem Website, 2014-2015, At Mogul

Introduction

Uppsalahem is the leading housing corporation in Uppsala and manages approximately 160 residential areas in the municipality of Uppsala with more than 14 800 apartments. The new website focuses on finding vacant apartments and providing digital services to Uppsalahem customers. I was responsible for the logical design and produced interactive prototypes that helped us communicate and test our design concepts. While working with this site we used a mobile first approach meaning that through out the logical design phase we always started designing for small screen sizes. It helped us prioritise and focus on key features.
